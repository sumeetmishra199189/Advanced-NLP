Peanut Butter Bars I
Recipe by : Nancy
Ingredients : 1 cup butter or margarine, melted2 cups graham cracker crumbs2 cups confectioners' sugar1 cup peanut butter1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips4 tablespoons peanut butter
Directions : In a medium bowl, mix together the butter or margarine, graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, and 1 cup peanut butter until well blended. Press evenly into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13 inch pan.
                            In a metal bowl over simmering water, or in the microwave, melt the chocolate chips with the peanut butter, stirring occasionally until smooth. Spread over the prepared crust. Refrigerate for at least one hour before cutting into squares.
