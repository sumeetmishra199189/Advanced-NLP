The Best Fresh Tomato Salsa
Recipe by : Cheryl Dressler
Ingredients : 3 cups chopped tomatoes1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper1 cup onion, diced1/4 cup minced fresh cilantro2 tablespoons fresh lime juice4 teaspoons chopped fresh jalapeno pepper (including seeds)1/2 teaspoon ground cumin1/2 teaspoon kosher salt1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
Directions : Stir the tomatoes, green bell pepper, onion, cilantro, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, cumin, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Serve.
