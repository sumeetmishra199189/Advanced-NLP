Slow Cooker Steak Fajitas
Recipe by : Miguel Ruiz
Ingredients : 1 (1 1/2-pound) beef flank steak1 (8 ounce) can chopped tomatoes1 onion, sliced1 green bell pepper, sliced into strips1 red bell pepper, sliced into strips1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped2 cloves garlic, minced1 teaspoon chili powder1 teaspoon ground cumin1 teaspoon ground coriander1/4 teaspoon salt6 large flour tortillas, or as needed1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
Directions : Cut flank steak into 6 equal portions and place in a slow cooker. Add tomatoes, onion, bell peppers, jalapeno pepper, garlic, chili powder, cumin, coriander, and salt.
                            Cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours, or on High for 4 to 6 hours, until steak is fall-apart-tender.
                            Remove steak and shred. Return to slow cooker.
                            Serve hot with tortillas and shredded cheese, allowing everyone to make their own fajita.
