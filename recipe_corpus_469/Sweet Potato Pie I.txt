Sweet Potato Pie I
Recipe by : COUGAAR
Ingredients : 1 (1 pound) sweet potato1/2 cup butter, softened1 cup white sugar1/2 cup milk2 eggs1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon1 teaspoon vanilla extract1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
Directions : Boil sweet potato whole in skin for 40 to 50 minutes, or until done.  Run cold water over the sweet potato, and remove the skin.
                                    Watch Now
Break apart sweet potato in a bowl.  Add butter, and mix well with mixer. Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until mixture is smooth.  Pour filling into an unbaked pie crust.
                                    Watch Now
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 to 60 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Pie will puff up like a souffle, and then will sink down as it cools.
