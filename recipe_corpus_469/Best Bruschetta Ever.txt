Best Bruschetta Ever
Recipe by : AZRIEL
Ingredients : 2 tomatoes, cubed1 teaspoon dried basil4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese2 tablespoons olive oil1 clove garlic, crushedseasoning salt to tasteground black pepper to taste
Directions : In a medium bowl, mix tomatoes, dried basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, garlic, seasoning salt and ground black pepper. Cover and chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.
