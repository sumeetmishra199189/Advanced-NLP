Mom's Best Waffles
Recipe by : Love
Ingredients : 2 cups all-purpose flour2 teaspoons baking powder2 tablespoons white sugar1 teaspoon salt2 cups milk2 eggs2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Directions : In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. Add milk, eggs and oil; mix well.
                            Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown.
