Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers
Recipe by : videogator
Ingredients : 1/2 cup cream cheese1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed12 slices bacon
Directions : Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
                            Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended. Fill each jalapeno half with the cheese mixture. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon. Arrange bacon-wrapped peppers on the prepared baking sheet.
                            Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, about 15 minutes.
