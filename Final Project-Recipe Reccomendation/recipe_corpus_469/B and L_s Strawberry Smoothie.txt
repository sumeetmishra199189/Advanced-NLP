B and L's Strawberry Smoothie
Recipe by : Lisa and Brittany
Ingredients : 8 strawberries, hulled1/2 cup skim milk1/2 cup plain yogurt3 tablespoons white sugar2 teaspoons vanilla extract6 cubes ice, crushed
Directions : In a blender combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar and vanilla. Toss in the ice. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses and serve.
