Cheese Fondue
Recipe by : Sheila
Ingredients : 1 cup dry white wine1/2 pound shredded Swiss cheese1/2 pound shredded Gruyere cheese2 tablespoons all-purpose flour1/4 teaspoon salt1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut into 1 inch cubes
Directions : Simmer wine in fondue pot. Add Swiss cheese, Gruyere cheese, 1/4 pound at a time. Stir after each addition of cheese until melted. Stir in flour. When all the cheese has melted, stir in salt and nutmeg. Serve with cut-up French bread.
