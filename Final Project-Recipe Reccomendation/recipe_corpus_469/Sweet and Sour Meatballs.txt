Sweet and Sour Meatballs
Recipe by : Tammy Moore
Ingredients : 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle chile sauce2 teaspoons lemon juice9 ounces grape jelly1 pound lean ground beef1 egg, beaten1 large onion, gratedsalt to taste
Directions : Whisk together the chili sauce, lemon juice and grape jelly. Pour into slow cooker and simmer over low heat until warm.
                            Combine ground beef, egg, onion and salt. Mix well and form into 1 inch balls. Add to sauce and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
