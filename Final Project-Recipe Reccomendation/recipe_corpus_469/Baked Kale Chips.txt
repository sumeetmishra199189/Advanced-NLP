Baked Kale Chips
Recipe by : LucyDelRey
Ingredients : 1 bunch kale1 tablespoon olive oil1 teaspoon seasoned salt
Directions : Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a non insulated cookie sheet with parchment paper.
                            With a knife or kitchen shears carefully remove the leaves from the thick stems and tear into bite size pieces. Wash and thoroughly dry kale with a salad spinner. Drizzle kale with olive oil and sprinkle with seasoning salt.
                            Bake until the edges brown but are not burnt, 10 to 15 minutes.
