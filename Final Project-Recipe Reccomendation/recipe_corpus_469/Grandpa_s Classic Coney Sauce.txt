Grandpa's Classic Coney Sauce
Recipe by : Sean S.
Ingredients : 2 pounds ground beef1/2 cup chopped onion1 1/2 cups ketchup1/4 cup white sugar1/4 cup white vinegar1/4 cup prepared yellow mustard1/2 teaspoon celery seed3/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper3/4 teaspoon salt
Directions : Place the ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook, stirring to crumble, until beef is browned. Drain. Transfer the beef and onion to a slow cooker and stir in the ketchup, sugar, vinegar and mustard. Season with celery seed, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt. Cover and simmer on Low setting for a few hours before serving.
