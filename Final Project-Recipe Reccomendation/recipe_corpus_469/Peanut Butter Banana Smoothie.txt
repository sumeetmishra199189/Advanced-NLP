Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie
Recipe by : Becca
Ingredients : 2 bananas, broken into chunks2 cups milk1/2 cup peanut butter2 tablespoons honey, or to taste2 cups ice cubes
Directions : Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
