Lola's Horchata
Recipe by : LOLA
Ingredients : 1 cup uncooked white long-grain rice5 cups water1/2 cup milk1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon2/3 cup white sugar
Directions : Pour the rice and water into the bowl of a blender; blend until the rice just begins to break up, about 1 minute. Let rice and water stand at room temperature for a minimum of 3 hours.
                                    Watch Now
Strain the rice water into a pitcher and discard the rice. Stir the milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and sugar into the rice water. Chill and stir before serving over ice.
