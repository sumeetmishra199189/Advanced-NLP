Classic Waffles
Recipe by : Megan
Ingredients : 2 cups all-purpose flour1 teaspoon salt4 teaspoons baking powder2 tablespoons white sugar2 eggs1 1/2 cups warm milk1/3 cup butter, melted1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Directions : In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
                            In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
                            Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
