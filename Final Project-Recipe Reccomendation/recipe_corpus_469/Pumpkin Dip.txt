Pumpkin Dip
Recipe by : SUE CASE
Ingredients : 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened2 cups confectioners' sugar1 (15 ounce) can solid pack pumpkin1 tablespoon ground cinnamon1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice1 teaspoon frozen orange juice concentrate
Directions : In a medium bowl, blend cream cheese and confectioners' sugar until smooth. Gradually mix in the pumpkin. Stir in the cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, and orange juice until smooth and well blended. Chill until serving.
