Sausage Brunch Casserole
Recipe by : JENNISELLERS
Ingredients : 1 1/2 pounds ground pork sausage1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough2 cups mozzarella cheese4 eggs, beaten3/4 cup milksalt and pepper to taste
Directions : Place sausage in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
                            Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan. Lay crescent rolls flat in the bottom of the pan. Combine cooked sausage, cheese, eggs, milk, salt and pepper; pour over crescent rolls.
                            Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, until bubbly and rolls are baked.
