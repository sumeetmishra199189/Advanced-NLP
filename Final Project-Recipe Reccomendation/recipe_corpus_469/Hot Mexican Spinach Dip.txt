Hot Mexican Spinach Dip
Recipe by : Linda
Ingredients : 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, diced and softened1 cup evaporated milk1 (2.25 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained1 tablespoon red wine vinegarsalt and pepper to taste
Directions : Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
                            In a medium baking dish, mix together salsa, chopped spinach, Monterey Jack cheese, cream cheese, evaporated milk, black olives, red wine vinegar, salt and pepper.
                            Bake mixture in the preheated oven 12 to 15 minutes, or until bubbly.
