Slow Cooker Green Bean Casserole
Recipe by : Hollus2@gmail.com
Ingredients : 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen cut green beans2 (10.75 ounce) cans cream of chicken soup2/3 cup milk1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese1/4 teaspoon salt1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper1 (6 ounce) can French-fried onions, divided
Directions : Combine green beans, cream of chicken soup, milk, Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper, and half the can of French-fried onions in a slow cooker.
                            Cover and cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours. Top casserole with remaining French-fried onions to serve.
