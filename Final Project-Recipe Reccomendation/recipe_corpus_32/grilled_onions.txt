 Grilled Onions/Cebollas Asadas
Submitted by: R.M. Chandler-Burns
Ingredients:
See Cooking Instructions.
Cooking Time: 	60 min
Units: 	US
Serves: 	2+
Cooking Instructions:
Place large white or Bermuda onion in sufficient aluminum paper to cover it completely, sealing and protecting it from the coals into which it will be placed. Check at about 60 minutes with a large fork. When onion has become soft, take out of the coals and remove the aluminum paper. Smother onions in fresh lime juice and add salt to taste.
