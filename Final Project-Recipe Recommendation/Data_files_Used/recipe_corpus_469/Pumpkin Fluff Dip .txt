Pumpkin Fluff Dip 
Recipe by : Tiera Lesley
Ingredients : 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix1 (15 ounce) can solid pack pumpkin1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
Directions : In a large bowl, mix together instant vanilla pudding mix, pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice. Fold in the thawed frozen whipped topping. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
