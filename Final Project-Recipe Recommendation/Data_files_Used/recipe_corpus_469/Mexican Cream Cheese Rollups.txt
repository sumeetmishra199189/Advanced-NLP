Mexican Cream Cheese Rollups
Recipe by : Kathy Jenkins
Ingredients : 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened1/3 cup mayonnaise2/3 cup pitted green olives, chopped1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped6 green onions, chopped8 (10 inch) flour tortillas1/2 cup salsa
Directions : In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise, green olives, black olives and green onions.
                            Spread cream cheese mixture in a thin layer onto each tortilla. Roll up tortillas.  Chill about 1 hour, or until the filling is firm.
                            Slice chilled rollups into 1 inch pieces. Serve with salsa for dipping.
