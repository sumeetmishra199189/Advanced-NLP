Carla's Fruit Dip
Recipe by : Clara Simons
Ingredients : 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese
Directions : In a mixing bowl, combine the frozen whipped topping, marshmallow creme, and cream cheese. Mix until smooth. Serve chilled.
