BLT Dip
Recipe by : Kathy W.
Ingredients : 1 pound bacon1 cup mayonnaise1 cup sour cream1 tomato - peeled, seeded and diced
Directions : Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain on paper towels.
                            In a medium bowl, combine mayonnaise and sour cream. Crumble bacon into the sour cream and mayonnaise mixture. Mix in tomatoes just before serving.
