Watermelon Fire and Ice Salsa
Recipe by : Dawn Suzie HomeMaker Hardy
Ingredients : 3 cups chopped watermelon1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper2 tablespoons lime juice2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro1 tablespoon chopped green onions1 tablespoon chopped jalapeno pepper1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
Directions : In a large bowl, combine the watermelon, green bell pepper, lime juice, cilantro, green onions, jalapeno and garlic salt. Mix well and serve.
