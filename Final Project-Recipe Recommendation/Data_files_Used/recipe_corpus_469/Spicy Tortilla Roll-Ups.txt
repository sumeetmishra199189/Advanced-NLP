Spicy Tortilla Roll-Ups
Recipe by : SHECOOKS2
Ingredients : 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles1 (4 ounce) jar sliced pimento peppers, drained2 green onions, minced3 tablespoons hot pepper sauce3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro10 (10 inch) flour tortillas
Directions : In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, olives, chiles, pimentos, green onions, hot sauce and fresh cilantro. Spread the mixture onto tortillas. Roll the tortillas up and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
                            Slice the roll ups and serve.
