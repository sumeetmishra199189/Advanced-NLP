Dominican Style Oatmeal
Recipe by : Pepita
Ingredients : 1 1/2 cups milk1/2 cup quick cooking oats2 tablespoons white sugar1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon1 pinch ground nutmeg1 pinch salt1/4 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
Directions : Combine milk, oats, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt in a saucepan. Add lemon peel, if using. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly for 2 minutes.
