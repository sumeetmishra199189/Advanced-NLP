Luscious Spinach Artichoke Dip
Recipe by : Youri
Ingredients : 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped1/2 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed1/2 cup sour cream1/4 cup mayonnaise1/4 cup cream cheese1/4 cup grated Romano cheese1/4 teaspoon minced garlic
Directions : Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
                            In a small baking dish, mix together artichoke hearts, spinach, sour cream, mayonnaise, cream cheese, Romano cheese, and garlic. Cover dish.
                            Bake until heated through and bubbly, about 25 minutes.
