Jalapeno Popper Spread
Recipe by : Denise Millar Ramsey
Ingredients : 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened1 cup mayonnaise1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained2 ounces canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Directions : Stir together cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles and jalapeno peppers. Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
                            Microwave on High until hot, about 3 minutes.
