Easy Pancakes
Recipe by : Sharon Holt
Ingredients : 1 cup all-purpose flour2 tablespoons white sugar2 teaspoons baking powder1 teaspoon salt1 egg, beaten1 cup milk2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Directions : In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.
                            Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
