Spiced Pumpkin Seeds
Recipe by : Carol
Ingredients : 1 1/2 tablespoons margarine, melted1/2 teaspoon salt1/8 teaspoon garlic salt2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce2 cups raw whole pumpkin seeds
Directions : Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
                            Combine the margarine, salt, garlic salt, Worcestershire sauce and pumpkin seeds. Mix thoroughly and place in shallow baking dish.
                            Bake for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
