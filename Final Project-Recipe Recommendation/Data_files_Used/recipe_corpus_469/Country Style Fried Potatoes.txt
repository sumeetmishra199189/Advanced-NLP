Country Style Fried Potatoes
Recipe by : sal
Ingredients : 1/3 cup shortening6 large potatoes, peeled and cubed1 teaspoon salt1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper1/2 teaspoon garlic powder1/2 teaspoon paprika
Directions : In a large cast iron skillet, heat shortening over medium-high heat. Add potatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are golden brown. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika. Serve hot.
