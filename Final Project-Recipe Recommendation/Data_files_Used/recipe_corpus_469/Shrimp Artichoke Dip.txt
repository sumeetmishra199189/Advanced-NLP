Shrimp Artichoke Dip
Recipe by : Kat
Ingredients : 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese1 cup grated Parmesan cheese1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained1/2 cup chopped green onions1/2 teaspoon garlic salt1/2 cup mayonnaise1 cup cooked and peeled shrimppaprika, for garnish
Directions : Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
                            In a medium baking dish, mix together Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, artichoke hearts, green onions, garlic salt, mayonnaise and shrimp. Sprinkle with paprika.
                            Bake in the preheated oven 20 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
