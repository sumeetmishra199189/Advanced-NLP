Fresh Tomato Salsa
Recipe by : bluebayou
Ingredients : 3 tomatoes, chopped1/2 cup finely diced onion5 serrano chiles, finely chopped1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro1 teaspoon salt2 teaspoons lime juice
Directions : In a medium bowl, stir together tomatoes, onion, chili peppers, cilantro, salt, and lime juice. Chill for one hour in the refrigerator before serving.
