Easy Cheese Ball II
Recipe by : EXPLORE725
Ingredients : 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix2 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
Directions : In a medium size bowl, mash cream cheese. Mix dressing mix and Cheddar cheese into the cream cheese. Shape the mixture into a ball. Roll the ball in the chopped nuts. Refrigerate covered until ready to serve. Yum!
