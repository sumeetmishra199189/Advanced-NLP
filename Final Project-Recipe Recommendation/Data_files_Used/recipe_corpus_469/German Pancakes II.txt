German Pancakes II
Recipe by : BRENNASPITZ
Ingredients : 1/4 cup butter1 cup all-purpose flour1 cup milk6 eggs, lightly beaten1/8 teaspoon salt
Directions : Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Melt butter in a medium baking dish.
                            In a medium bowl, mix flour, milk, eggs and salt. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
                            Bake on center rack in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden brown.
