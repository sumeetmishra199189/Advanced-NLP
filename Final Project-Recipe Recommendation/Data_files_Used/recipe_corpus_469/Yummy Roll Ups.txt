Yummy Roll Ups
Recipe by : Courtney McDonald
Ingredients : 5 slices cooked ham1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened5 dill pickle spears
Directions : Lay the ham slices flat on a serving plate and pat dry. Spread with cream cheese. Place a pickle spear at one end of each slice, and roll the slices into cylinders around the spears; secure with toothpicks.
