French Toast I
Recipe by : Jan Bittner
Ingredients : 6 thick slices bread2 eggs2/3 cup milk1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)salt to taste
Directions : Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spices and vanilla.
                            Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
                            Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides.  Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden.  Serve hot.
