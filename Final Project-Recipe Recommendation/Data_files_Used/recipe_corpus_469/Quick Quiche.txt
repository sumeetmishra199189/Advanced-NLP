Quick Quiche
Recipe by : myrite
Ingredients : 8 slices bacon4 ounces shredded Swiss cheese2 tablespoons butter, melted4 eggs, beaten1/4 cup finely chopped onion1 teaspoon salt1/2 cup all-purpose flour1 1/2 cups milk
Directions : Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside.
                            Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
                            Line bottom of pie plate with cheese and crumbled bacon. Combine eggs, butter, onion, salt, flour and milk; whisk together until smooth; pour into pie pan.
                            Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, until set. Serve hot or cold.
