Artichoke Spinach Dip
Recipe by : CINDYJ60
Ingredients : 1/4 cup butter1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, partially thawed1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened2 (16 ounce) containers sour cream1 cup grated Parmesan cheesegarlic salt to taste
Directions : Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the spinach and artichoke hearts. Cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
                            Mix cream cheese and sour cream into the spinach mixture. Stir in Parmesan cheese and garlic salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, 10 to 15 minutes. Serve warm.
