Fried Cabbage with Bacon, Onion, and Garlic
Recipe by : Kathi Richards Smith
Ingredients : 6 slices bacon, chopped1 large onion, diced2 cloves garlic, minced1 large head cabbage, cored and sliced1 tablespoon salt, or to taste1 teaspoon ground black pepper1/2 teaspoon onion powder1/2 teaspoon garlic powder1/8 teaspoon paprika
Directions : Place the bacon in a large stockpot and cook over medium-high heat until crispy, about 10 minutes. Add the onion and garlic; cook and stir until the onion caramelizes; about 10 minutes. Immediately stir in the cabbage and continue to cook and stir another 10 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, and paprika. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer, stirring occasionally, about 30 minutes more.
